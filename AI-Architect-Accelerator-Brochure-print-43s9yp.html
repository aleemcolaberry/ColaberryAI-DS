Intensive Deep Dives

Intensive Deep Dives

What each intensive delivers

1 · Build Your AI FoundationWeeks 1–3

Set up the workspace and habits of a working AI systems architect, then ship your first automation. You build a configured Architect Workspace, three reusable AI Skills in a domain you choose, and a working Business Workflow Assistant connected to one external API.

You leave with a workspace reusable on any future project, a portfolio-ready Skills library, and an assistant you can use immediately.

Prerequisites: basic software concepts (git, command line) and a business problem to address.

2 · Create Your AI TeamWeeks 4–6

Move from a single assistant to a coordinated team of AI agents. You build an Enterprise Prompt Library, a multi-agent system of at least three specialized agents that coordinate on a complex task (for example a Compliance Agent, an Audit Agent, and a Reviewer Agent), and documented coordination patterns.

You leave with a reusable prompt library, a multi-agent architecture you can extend, and pattern documentation strong enough for technical interviews.

3 · Connect AI To The Real WorldWeeks 7–9

Give your AI access to real business data through the Model Context Protocol (MCP), a scarce and in-demand skill. You build a working MCP server, at least one integration with a business system you select (SQL database, document store, CRM, calendar, or email), and an end-to-end flow showing AI consuming real data.

You leave with a reusable MCP server and a real integration suitable for production or portfolio use.

4 · Design AI That ScalesWeeks 10–12

Take an AI system from "it works" to "it is reliable, governed, and ready for production review." You build an AI Reliability Framework (validation, retry logic, confidence scoring), an AI Governance Engine (audit logging, human approval flows, escalation paths), and a complete Solution Architecture Package.

You leave with reusable frameworks and a polished package suitable for executive review, technical interviews, or RFP submissions.

Common Questions

Common Questions

Answers before you ask

Is this worth $149 a month?

Your membership is not a course fee. For one monthly price you get live training in all four intensives, Anthropic Architect certification prep, a full-time internship, and guided portfolio projects. Compare that to a traditional bootcamp charging ten to fifteen thousand dollars up front for less.

I work full time — can I keep up?

Live time is four hours a week: Monday and Thursday, two hours each, for 12 weeks. You build your own project between sessions at your own pace, and because the project is yours, the build time doubles as real work.

Am I technical enough?

You need comfort with basic software concepts like git and the command line, a business problem you want to solve, and the willingness to build. You do not need to be a professional engineer.

What if it is not for me?

On the monthly plan, cancel anytime. On the annual plan, a 7-day refund window from your start date lets you begin, see how it runs, and decide with no risk. (Exact annual refund terms pending confirmation.)

Why now?
